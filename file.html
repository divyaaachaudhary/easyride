FAQ
What types of vehicles are available with Easy Ride?
Easy Ride offers a variety of vehicles to suit different mobility needs, including vans with wheelchair lifts and ramps, sedans with ample space for mobility aids, and vehicles equipped with sensory-friendly features. Each vehicle is regularly inspected and maintained to ensure safety and comfort.
How do I book a ride with Easy Ride?
You can book a ride through our website or mobile app. Our booking system is designed to be accessible, with features like voice recognition and high-contrast visuals for those with visual impairments. Simply enter your pick-up and drop-off locations, select your preferred vehicle type, and choose your travel date and time.
Can I cancel or reschedule my ride?
Yes, you can easily cancel or reschedule your ride through our app or website. We understand that plans can change, so we offer flexible cancellation policies. Please try to inform us at least 24 hours in advance if you need to cancel or reschedule, to avoid any charges.
Are there trained professionals available to assist during my ride?
Absolutely! All our drivers and assistants are trained in disability awareness and assistance techniques. They are equipped to help you from the moment they arrive for pick-up until you are safely at your destination. If you require specific assistance, please specify this when booking your ride.
What safety measures does Easy Ride implement?
Safety is our top priority. All our vehicles are equipped with necessary safety features like securement straps for wheelchairs and other mobility devices. Our drivers are all thoroughly vetted and trained in defensive driving and emergency response. Additionally, our vehicles are regularly sanitized and maintained to meet high safety and hygiene standards.
Can family members or caregivers travel with me?
Yes, family members or caregivers are welcome to accompany you during your ride, provided there is sufficient space in the chosen vehicle. Please indicate how many people will be traveling with you when you book your ride so we can accommodate everyone comfortably.
